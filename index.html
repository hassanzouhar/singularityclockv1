Inspired by "Bulletin of the Atomic Scientists" we want to build a prediction engine for humanity. Crazy right? But we have a problem, the clock is broken. It does not have enough data to function properly, but we are working on it.

Understanding Human and AI Co-Evolution

Our goal is to build a comprehensive platform to track and try to predict the outcomes of the introduction of broad AI into our society. Through analysis of open source data like employment trends, investment patterns, and a substantial collection of economic indicators, coupled with tried and true predictive models, like Moores law, we aim to provide real-time insights into AI affects society, business and humanity .

How do we gain insight?

Crowdsourcing Feedback: Gathering insights from workers in diverse industries.

Predictive Modeling: Applying laws like Moore’s Law to forecast AI advancements.

Employment Trends: Monitoring shifts in employment and unemployment rates across sectors.

Consumer Trends: Tracking where money is flowing in the AI space.

Economic Indicators: Utilizing macro and microeconomic data to gauge AI’s impact.
