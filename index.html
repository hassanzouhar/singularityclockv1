Predicting the Future of AI Adoption

We are building a comprehensive platform to track and predict the outcomes of the introduction of broad AI into society. Through meticulous analysis of employment trends, investment patterns, and various economic indicators, coupled with Moore's Law and other predictive models, we aim to provide real-time insights into how quickly AI adoption is accelerating.

What We Do

Employment Analysis: Monitoring shifts in employment and unemployment rates across sectors.

Investment Trends: Tracking where money is flowing in the AI space.

Economic Indicators: Utilizing macro and microeconomic data to gauge AI’s impact.

Predictive Modeling: Applying laws like Moore’s Law to forecast AI advancements.

Crowdsourcing Feedback: Gathering insights from workers in diverse industries.
